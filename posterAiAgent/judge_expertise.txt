Bing Dong's research focuses on intelligent building operations, modeling and simulation of occupancy behavior in buildings, and the development of control strategies for building energy systems.  He has over 20 years of experience in smart building systems, building energy performance simulation, and occupancy behavior modeling.  His work utilizes Building Information Modeling (BIM) and explores how smart buildings actively participate in urban-scale cyber-physical energy systems.  He has published over 120 peer-reviewed papers, cited over 8,000 times.  He is an IBPSA (International Building Performance Simulation Association) World Fellow, recognizing his significant contributions to the field.  His expertise includes areas such as building energy performance simulation, occupancy behavior modeling, BIM, and control strategies for building energy systems.
